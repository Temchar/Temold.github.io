Intro

Hello, my name is Wyatt Smith, and I am a graduating senior at The Dayton Regional STEM School. This digital Portfolio is a record of My Success' and Challenges throughout my 5 years at STEM. My About me page is a way to get to know a bit more about my life outside school, and my experiences tab can help with knowing what I have achieved throughout the years. My projects tabs shows how I've grown since my first days at STEM.

Below you can find more about me, and links at the bottom to important exterior pages.



Robotics

I have been doing robotics through FIRST since 7th grade, and although have struggled a lot in those 5 years, I have also learned alot. The FIRST program has taught me alot about Leadership, team cooperation, and the engineering process. It has given me a chance to express my creativity in a format that mixed well with how I think. I feel the FIRST program and my team have helped shape who I am today.




Scouting

Although robotics has had a significant impact on my life, my scouting life has been one of my longest experiences. I have been in scouting since the 1st grade, and I feel that it has truly helped me discover myself. It definitely hasnt always been a positive thing in my life, however I probably wouldnt be at this school if it had not been for the people I met, and experiences I had while in scouting. I am currently a life scout in the program, and intend to make eagle before I turn 18.
