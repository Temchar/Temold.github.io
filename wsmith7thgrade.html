STILL PUSHING THROUGH

“Rules are for the obedience of fools and the guidance of men”, Douglas Bader. Hello, I am Wyatt Smith. Welcome to my portfolio presentation of my lab safety project. Throughout the year, I have grown in many things, one of them being persistence. Persistence is pushing through when things get hard. I have gotten better at persistence through the lab safety project.

The lab safety project is a project in which we had to create a poster to show one of the lab safety rules without using any words. We had to, portray the rule accurately, not have any words, and the final draft must fit on the required paper size. Throughout the project I faced a lot of challenges, however the one that kept popping up was that my lines weren’t strait. Also it was hard to understand. However as I persisted on and I completed more drafts my lines got neater and cleaner. In addition, at the end I feel I had grown a lot in persistance.

This year I have grown in many ways, one of my greatest growths is in persistence. This project helped me to be neater by having me place the pieces just right to show the lab safety rule. I feel this will help me have more craftsmanship later on because you need to be neat to get into a high ranking job. This can also be applied to other people.
